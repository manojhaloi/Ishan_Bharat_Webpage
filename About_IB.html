Welcome to Ishan Bharat

Ishan Bharat is a Guwahati based trust dedicated to the people of Assam and North east India. The area of operation of the trust will cover all the eight North eastern states of India, i.e Arunachal Pradesh, Assam, Manipur, Meghalaya, Mizoram, Nagaland, Sikkim and Tripura. However, the area of operation can widen according to requirement.

The trust is cofounded by Dr. Kanak Haloi, former professor and Head, CGARD, NIRD&PR, Guwahati and Dr. Jonali Devi, Associate Professor, Department of Anthropology, Cotton University. The trust is run by its managing committee.

The aim and objectives behind the establishment of the trust are as follows:

  1. To act as forum towards framing local, sub regional and regional level policies development and policy advocacy related to the issues of sustainable social, economic, natural, human and cultural resources etc which the TRUST consider desirable and necessary.

  2. To setup and act as in information service center towards enhancing easy access of citizen centric information, geospatial information and socio-cultural information etc which the TRUST consider desirable and necessary.

  3. To establish and act as village reconstruction center by promoting and associating interested village level partner (s)and to undertake village adoption programme with the aims of transforming the villages to the level of model village by acting in the areas of local institution promotion, cleanliness, healthy habit, skill development/ up-graduation, drinking water, sanitation, entrepreneurship promotion, empowerment, health, education, income generation, natural resources management including wetlands, common property resources management etc. Which the TRUST consider desirable and necessary.

  4. To set up and act as research and training Center to undertake and conduct
      (a) Research/ consultancy studies/projects related to society, culture, institution, economics, natural resources including wetlands, common property resources, environment, project planning, implementation, monitoring and evaluation,
      (b) Awareness and training on the institutions of poor producers groups, livelihoods, local institutions, Panchayat raj institutions, drinking water, sanitation skill up gradation/ development, entrepreneurship promotion, empowerment, ICTS including geo-informatics, project formulation and planning , natural resources management including wetlands,
      (c) Cultural exchange programme and exposure visit programme and
      (d) Award certificate and diploma and also give stipend and scholarship etc which the TRUST considers desirable and necessary.

  5. To print, publish and distribute gratuitously or otherwise books, reports, leaflets and journal which the trust may consider desirable and necessary for the promotion of its objectives.

  6. To undertake and conduct some or all activities include under objectives no. 1, 2, 3, 4 , and 5 by the trust of its own or in collaboration, or by obtaining grant/sponsorship from government/ semi government/ corporate bodies or by funds generation through donation which the TRUST considers desirable and necessary towards attainment of its objectives.
