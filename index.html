About Ishan Bharat

It is a Trust founded in the year 2015 at Guwahati, Assam primarily to work in India's North East and bordering nations. The trust envisions for working collectively and collaboratively towards bringing about transformation and development at the habitat level of the people and at the same time evolve initiatives, build strategies and processes in the form of literature and documents for wider dissemination and sharing.
